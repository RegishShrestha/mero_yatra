Nenyul Village,Mustang District

Nenyul village lies at the height of 3880 meters from the sea level. It is about 45 minutes from Lo-Manthang. When you hike to Kunchokling or Samjong you you will pass this village.

Tsum valley

The Tsum Valley is one of the most secluded valleys in Nepal. Situated in the Manaslu region, high up near the Tibetan border, the whole area has a definite Tibetan Buddhist feel to it and is quite different from the surrounding countryside.
